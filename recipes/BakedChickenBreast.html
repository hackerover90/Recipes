Baked Chicken Breast and Roasted Potatoes

Nice and juicy chicken. This is the go to meal for all body builders.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast
    • Seasoning Mix
      • Paprika
      • Salt
      • Garlic Powder
      • Chilli Powder
      • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • 500 grams of Yellow Potatoes
    • Seasoning Mix
      • Salt
      • Paprika
      • Cayenne Pepper
      • Oregano
      • 1 tsp Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees farenheit
  2. Cut pototaoes into cubes
  3. Mix potatoes with seasonings
  4. Mix chicken with seasonings
  5. Place baking sheet on a baking tray and place chicken and potatoes on the tray
  6. Place tray in the oven for 16 minutes
  7. Let it cool for 5 minutes and enjoy!
