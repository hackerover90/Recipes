Scrambled Eggs and Toast

A good protein rich breakfast. Very tasty and easy to make as well as a low ingredient count.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • 1 Slice of Whole Wheat Bread

Steps

  1. Set pan to medium high heat and add oil to the pan
  2. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl
  3. Add salt and whisk bowl
  4. Pour out bowl into pan
  5. Stir until eggs are cooked to your preference
  6. Toast bread if desired
  7. Enjoy!
