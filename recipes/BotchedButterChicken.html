Botched Butter Chicken

A delicious recipe, perfect for meal prepping and very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 750 grams of Chicken Breast
  • Seasonings:
    • 1.75 tbsp Garlic Powder
    • 2.5 tbsp Paprika
    • 2.5 tsp Garam Masala
    • 2.5 tsp Turmeric
    • 1.25 tsp Chilli Powder
    • 2.5 tsp Salt
    • 2.5 tsp Pepper
  • 1 Lemon
  • 250 g Fat Free Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Big Onion or 2 Small Onions
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into cubes
  2. Mix chicken with seasonings, lemon and greek yogurt
  3. Set pan to medium high heat and add olive oil and chopped onions
  4. Mix until onions are caramalized
  5. Add chicken mix consistently until chicken is cooked
  6. Let it cool before serving!
